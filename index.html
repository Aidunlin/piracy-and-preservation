Piracy and Preservation

An open letter on video game piracy, Nintendo's misguided efforts to combat it, and an alternative solution.

Dear Nintendo

Piracy is a problem. It is a massive, worldwide issue that plagues all game developers, companies, and studios. When someone pirates a game or illegally copies and shares a game over the internet, the dedicated team (and company) behind that game gets absolutely nothing in return. According to your website:

“Nintendo and its over 100 independent publishers and developers, lose hundreds of millions of dollars per year to international pirates and counterfeiters.”
